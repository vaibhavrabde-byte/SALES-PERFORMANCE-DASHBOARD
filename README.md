## Dashboard Overview

The **Sales Performance Dashboard** provides a comprehensive visual analysis of transaction data, tracking patterns across time, payment types, and daily operational hours.

### Visualizations & Metrics

* **Overall Volume (KPI Card):** A central metric showing a total **Sum of date** reaching **166M**, serving as the primary volume indicator for the dataset.
* **Card vs. Cash Analysis (Area & Pie Charts):**
* An **Area Chart** breaks down the *Sum of date by card and cash_type*, showing distinct spikes and distributions based on transaction methods.
* A detailed **Pie Chart** correlates the *Sum of date by card and cash_type*, illustrating how specific anonymized card segments contribute to the overall transaction value.


* **Temporal Distribution (Pie & Combo Charts):**
* A **Donut/Pie Chart** evaluates the *Sum of Monthsort and Sum of Weekdaysort by Time of Day and Weekday*, breaking activities down by **Morning**, **Afternoon**, and **Night** segments.
* A **Stacked Column and Line Combo Chart** displays the *Sum of Weekdaysort and Sum of Monthsort by Time_of_Day and Weekday*, mapping variations across different periods of the day.


* **Yearly & Monthly Trends (Scatter Matrix):**
* A multi-pane **Scatter Plot Matrix** tracks the *Sum of hour_day and Sum of money by Year and Month_name* (specifically highlighting April, August, December, and February across 2024 and 2025) to uncover seasonal performance shifts.
